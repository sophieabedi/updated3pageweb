What is a queen bee?

A queen bee is the one that produces eggs. She can lay as many as 2,000 eggs every day. Queen bees are only useful for about 2-3 years but can live for 5 years. They produce substances such as the queen substance which is a "mixture of chemicals" that "is passed individually from bee to bee throughout the entire hive as they share food."


What is a drone bee?

Drone bees are larger bees whose primary purpose is to mate with the queen They compete with other male bees to mate with the queen, and is one of the most important parts of a bee colony. Drone bees die when worker bees kick them out of the hive.


What is a worker bee?

Worker bees are predominately female and control what goes on inside the hive as well as do most of the work They live for about 6 weeks but do a lot of work during their lives such as feeding the queens, collecting pollen and housekeeping.
